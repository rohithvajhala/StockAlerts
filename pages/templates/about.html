StockAlerts

Stock alerts is an open source website where users can fetch various US based companies basic information and stock values. Users can create a profile and subscribe to their favorite stocks. Once subscribed, user will be able to get the alerts when their favorite stock price drops/raises depending on the chosen limits. This application uses open source API finnhub.io to get the information related to stocks.

How to subscribe to stocks?

  1. Create an account here and login to your account.
  2. Once logged in, on the home page you can search for your favorite stock.
  3. It shows the list of stocks based on your search query. Click on the stock you like and you can see more details related to the company.
  4. When you scroll to the bottom of the stock details you can see the subscribe button.
  5. Once you click subscribe button it will ask you to fill the lower and higher limits for the stock. (You can leave the fields empty if you wish).
  6. After selecting the limit check option to receive updates.

Now you will receive a mail as soon as the stock price drops below your selected lower limit or raises above your selected higher limit.

Want to update your limits for the stock turnoff alerts?

You can easily manage the limits / delete your subscribed stock by going to "WatchList" tab. "WatchList" tab provides you an overview of your subscribed stocks. {% endblock %}
